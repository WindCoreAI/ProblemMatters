# Industry Problem Domains Research

## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive research on industry-specific problems and challenges across major domains. The goal is to identify significant problems that can be cataloged, searched, and addressed through our AI-powered problem-finding engine.

---

## 1. Technology & Software Engineering

### 1.1 Software Development

**Major Challenges:**
- **Technical Debt Accumulation**: Legacy systems becoming unmaintainable, estimated to cost $1.52 trillion globally
- **Security Vulnerabilities**: Average cost of data breach is $4.45 million; supply chain attacks increasing
- **Developer Productivity**: Context switching, tooling fragmentation, cognitive overload
- **Quality Assurance**: Testing coverage gaps, regression detection, performance monitoring

**Sub-domains:**
- Frontend Development: Cross-browser compatibility, performance optimization, accessibility
- Backend Development: Scalability, distributed systems complexity, data consistency
- DevOps/Platform: Infrastructure management, deployment reliability, observability
- AI/ML Engineering: Model drift, training data quality, explainability

### 1.2 Cybersecurity

**Major Challenges:**
- **Talent Shortage**: 3.5 million unfilled cybersecurity positions globally
- **Evolving Threat Landscape**: AI-powered attacks, zero-day exploits, ransomware-as-a-service
- **Compliance Complexity**: GDPR, CCPA, HIPAA, SOC2 requirements across jurisdictions
- **Supply Chain Security**: Third-party vulnerabilities, software bill of materials (SBOM)

### 1.3 Semiconductors & Hardware

**Major Challenges:**
- **Manufacturing Complexity**: Sub-3nm process nodes, extreme UV lithography costs ($150M+ per machine)
- **Supply Chain Concentration**: 90% of advanced chips from Taiwan/South Korea
- **Design Complexity**: Billions of transistors, verification challenges
- **Sustainability**: Energy consumption of fabs, rare earth material dependencies
- **Geopolitical Risks**: Export controls, technology transfer restrictions

**Sub-domains:**
- Chip Design: Power efficiency, thermal management, 3D packaging
- Manufacturing: Yield optimization, defect detection, clean room operations
- Testing & Validation: At-scale testing, reliability prediction

## 2. Energy & Utilities

### 2.1 Renewable Energy

**Major Challenges:**
- **Intermittency**: Solar/wind variability requires grid flexibility and storage
- **Grid Integration**: Legacy infrastructure incompatible with distributed generation
- **Storage Technology**: Battery cost, lifespan, and recycling challenges
- **Land Use Conflicts**: NIMBYism, environmental impact of large installations

**Sub-domains:**
- Solar: Panel efficiency limits, degradation, recycling end-of-life panels
- Wind: Offshore installation costs, bird/bat mortality, noise complaints
- Hydro: Environmental impact, drought vulnerability, dam safety
- Nuclear: Waste storage, public perception, regulatory burden
- Geothermal: Geographic limitations, drilling costs, induced seismicity

### 2.2 Grid Infrastructure

**Major Challenges:**
- **Aging Infrastructure**: US grid averages 40+ years old, $2.5 trillion upgrade needed
- **Cybersecurity**: Critical infrastructure attacks, SCADA vulnerabilities
- **Load Balancing**: EV adoption surge, electrification of heating
- **Resilience**: Extreme weather events, wildfire risks

### 2.3 Energy Efficiency

**Major Challenges:**
- **Building Stock**: 40% of energy used in buildings, slow retrofit rates
- **Industrial Processes**: High-heat applications difficult to electrify
- **Behavioral Change**: Consumer awareness and motivation gaps
- **Measurement & Verification**: Quantifying savings, split incentives

## 3. Healthcare & Life Sciences

### 3.1 Drug Discovery & Development

**Major Challenges:**
- **R&D Costs**: Average $2.6 billion per approved drug, 10-15 year timeline
- **Clinical Trial Failures**: 90% failure rate from Phase I to approval
- **Rare Disease Neglect**: Limited commercial incentive for small patient populations
- **Personalized Medicine**: Biomarker discovery, companion diagnostics

### 3.2 Healthcare Delivery

**Major Challenges:**
- **Provider Burnout**: 63% of physicians report burnout symptoms
- **Administrative Burden**: 30% of healthcare spending is administrative
- **Access Disparities**: Rural healthcare deserts, socioeconomic barriers
- **Interoperability**: EHR fragmentation, data exchange standards

**Sub-domains:**
- Primary Care: Shortage of providers, preventive care gaps
- Mental Health: Stigma, access, integration with physical health
- Emergency Services: Overcrowding, response times, resource allocation
- Long-term Care: Aging population, caregiver shortage, quality metrics

### 3.3 Medical Devices & Diagnostics

**Major Challenges:**
- **Regulatory Pathways**: FDA clearance timelines, international harmonization
- **Cybersecurity**: Connected device vulnerabilities, legacy equipment
- **Affordability**: High costs limiting access, reimbursement challenges
- **User Error**: Training requirements, interface design

## 4. Financial Services

### 4.1 Banking & Payments

**Major Challenges:**
- **Legacy Systems**: Core banking platforms 30-40 years old
- **Financial Inclusion**: 1.4 billion adults globally unbanked
- **Fraud Prevention**: Real-time detection, synthetic identity fraud
- **Cross-border Payments**: High fees, slow settlement, compliance complexity

### 4.2 Insurance

**Major Challenges:**
- **Climate Risk Modeling**: Increasing frequency and severity of claims
- **Underwriting Accuracy**: Data quality, bias in algorithms
- **Customer Experience**: Claims processing friction, policy complexity
- **Cyber Insurance**: Pricing uncertainty, accumulation risk

### 4.3 Capital Markets

**Major Challenges:**
- **Market Manipulation**: Algorithmic trading risks, flash crashes
- **Settlement Efficiency**: T+1/T+0 settlement requirements
- **Regulatory Compliance**: MiFID II, Dodd-Frank, Basel III/IV
- **ESG Integration**: Standardized metrics, greenwashing detection

## 5. Manufacturing & Industrial

### 5.1 Advanced Manufacturing

**Major Challenges:**
- **Workforce Skills Gap**: 2.1 million unfilled manufacturing jobs by 2030
- **Supply Chain Resilience**: Single points of failure, visibility gaps
- **Quality Control**: Zero-defect demands, traceability requirements
- **Sustainability**: Circular economy transition, emissions reduction

**Sub-domains:**
- Automotive: EV transition, autonomous systems, software-defined vehicles
- Aerospace: Certification complexity, supply chain concentration, decarbonization
- Consumer Electronics: Product lifecycle shortening, e-waste, conflict minerals
- Heavy Industry: Decarbonization of steel, cement, chemicals

### 5.2 Industrial Automation

**Major Challenges:**
- **OT/IT Convergence**: Security risks, interoperability challenges
- **Predictive Maintenance**: Data quality, model accuracy, ROI justification
- **Human-Robot Collaboration**: Safety standards, job displacement concerns
- **Edge Computing**: Latency requirements, device management at scale

## 6. Agriculture & Food Systems

### 6.1 Precision Agriculture

**Major Challenges:**
- **Digital Divide**: Small farmers lack access to technology and capital
- **Data Ownership**: Who controls farm data, interoperability standards
- **Soil Health**: Degradation, carbon sequestration measurement
- **Water Management**: Irrigation efficiency, groundwater depletion

### 6.2 Food Supply Chain

**Major Challenges:**
- **Food Waste**: 30-40% of food production lost or wasted
- **Traceability**: Food safety recalls, origin verification
- **Cold Chain**: Last-mile delivery, developing world infrastructure
- **Food Security**: Price volatility, climate impacts on yields

### 6.3 Alternative Proteins

**Major Challenges:**
- **Scale-up**: Cultivated meat production costs and capacity
- **Consumer Acceptance**: Taste, texture, cultural barriers
- **Regulatory Approval**: Novel food safety frameworks
- **Nutrition Equivalence**: Complete protein profiles, bioavailability

- **Warehouse Automation**: Labor shortages, order complexity
- **Visibility & Tracking**: Real-time location, condition monitoring

## 10. Construction & Real Estate

### 10.1 Construction Industry

**Major Challenges:**
- **Productivity Stagnation**: Only 1% annual productivity growth over 20 years
- **Labor Shortage**: Skilled trades gap, aging workforce
- **Safety**: Construction accounts for 20% of worker fatalities
- **Project Delivery**: Cost overruns, schedule delays, disputes

**Sub-domains:**
- Residential: Housing affordability, NIMBYism, zoning constraints
- Commercial: Remote work impact, adaptive reuse
- Infrastructure: Maintenance backlog, funding mechanisms
- Modular/Prefab: Quality perception, transportation logistics

### 10.2 Building Operations

**Major Challenges:**
- **Energy Efficiency**: Existing building stock upgrades
- **Indoor Air Quality**: Post-pandemic expectations, sick building syndrome
- **Smart Building Integration**: Protocol fragmentation, cybersecurity
- **Maintenance Optimization**: Reactive to predictive transition

### Problem Classification Framework

| Attribute | Description | Example Values |
|-----------|-------------|----------------|
| Domain | Primary industry | Technology, Healthcare, Energy |
| Scope | Geographic/market scope | Global, Regional, Local |
| Maturity | Problem lifecycle stage | Emerging, Active, Chronic |
| Impact | Severity assessment | Critical, High, Medium, Low |
